TRAILHEAD GEAR CO. — RETURNS & EXCHANGES POLICY
Version 2.1 | Effective: 2026-01-01

1. RETURN WINDOW
We accept returns within 60 (sixty) days of the delivery date for most items. The
return window is measured from the date the order was delivered, not the date it was
placed or shipped. Orders that have not yet been delivered are not eligible to begin a
return; please wait until the package arrives. If 60 days have passed since delivery, the
item is outside the standard return window and is no longer eligible for a refund, though
it may still be covered under the Warranty Policy for manufacturing defects.

2. CONDITION REQUIREMENTS
Items must be returned unused, in original condition, with all tags attached and in their
original packaging. Footwear and apparel may be tried on indoors but must show no signs of
outdoor use, dirt, or wear. Tents, sleeping bags, and backpacks must be clean, dry, and
free of odor. We reserve the right to refuse a refund or to apply a restocking fee of up
to 15% for items returned in diminished condition.

3. NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
The following are final sale and cannot be returned: gift cards, clearance items marked
"Final Sale," used climbing protection and safety equipment, consumables (fuel canisters,
freeze-dried food), and any item that has been visibly used outdoors or damaged through
use rather than a manufacturing defect. Worn footwear and laundered base layers are not
returnable for hygiene reasons.

4. HOW TO START A RETURN
To begin a return, provide your order ID and the item you wish to return. We will confirm
eligibility against the delivery date and condition rules above and issue a prepaid return
shipping label. Pack the item securely and drop it at any authorized carrier location.
Returns must be postmarked within 14 days of the return label being issued.

5. REFUND METHOD AND TIMELINE
Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Once we receive and inspect the
returned item, refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days. Original shipping charges
are non-refundable except where the return is the result of our error or a defective item.
Summit Club members receive refunds to their original payment method or, optionally, as
store credit with a 5% bonus.

6. EXCHANGES
To exchange an item for a different size, color, or model, start a standard return and
place a new order for the replacement. If the exchange is for the same item in a different
size and stock is available, contact support and we will expedite the replacement at no
additional shipping cost.

7. DEFECTIVE OR DAMAGED ITEMS
Items that arrive damaged or that fail due to a manufacturing defect are handled under the
Warranty Policy and are not subject to the 60-day return window. Report defects with photos
and your order ID. See warranty-policy.txt for coverage terms by product category.

8. CONTACT
For return questions not answered here, contact the Trailhead Help Desk at
support@trailheadgear.example or call 1-800-555-TREK during business hours.

TRAILHEAD GEAR CO. — SHIPPING POLICY
Version 1.4 | Effective: 2026-01-01

1. PROCESSING TIME
Orders are processed and shipped within 1 to 2 business days of being placed. Orders placed
after 1:00 PM Pacific or on weekends and holidays begin processing the next business day.
You will receive a shipment confirmation email with tracking once your order ships. An
order in "placed" status has not yet shipped; an order in "shipped" status is in transit.

2. DOMESTIC SHIPPING METHODS AND COSTS
We offer three domestic (United States) shipping options:
   - Standard (5-7 business days): $7.95, or FREE on orders of $99 or more.
   - Expedited (2-3 business days): $16.95.
   - Overnight (next business day): $34.95.
Free standard shipping is applied automatically at checkout when your order subtotal,
before tax, is $99 or more.

3. DELIVERY TIMES
Delivery time is the processing time plus the carrier transit time for the method selected.
Standard orders typically arrive 6 to 9 business days after the order is placed. Remote
areas (Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories) may require 2 to 4 additional business days.

4. INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
We ship to Canada and most of Europe. International orders ship via tracked courier and
typically arrive in 7 to 14 business days. International shipping is calculated at checkout
based on destination and weight. The customer is responsible for any import duties, taxes,
or customs fees assessed by the destination country.

5. SUMMIT CLUB SHIPPING BENEFITS
Trail and Summit tier members receive free standard shipping on all orders with
no minimum. Summit tier members also receive free expedited shipping. See
membership-program.txt for tier details.

6. LOST OR DELAYED SHIPMENTS
If your tracking shows no movement for 7 or more business days, or a package is marked
delivered but not received, contact support with your order ID so we can open a carrier
investigation. Claims for lost shipments must be filed within 30 days of the ship date.

7. ADDRESS ACCURACY
We are not responsible for orders shipped to an incorrect address provided at checkout.
Please verify your shipping address before completing your order. Address changes can only
be made while an order is in "placed" status, before it ships.

8. CONTACT
For shipping questions, contact the Trailhead Help Desk at support@trailheadgear.example.

TRAILHEAD GEAR CO. — WARRANTY POLICY
Version 1.2 | Effective: 2026-01-01

1. OVERVIEW
Trailhead Gear Co. warrants its products against defects in materials and workmanship for
the periods described below. This warranty is separate from the 60-day return window in the
Returns Policy: warranty claims address manufacturing defects and may be filed after the
return window has closed. The warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, damage from
misuse, accidents, improper care, or alterations.

2. LIFETIME LIMITED WARRANTY — PACKS AND TENTS
Backpacks and tents carry a limited lifetime warranty covering manufacturing defects in
materials and workmanship for the practical lifetime of the product. Zippers, buckles,
seams, and pole hubs that fail under normal use are repaired or replaced at our discretion.
This warranty does not cover UV degradation, abrasion, or damage from improper storage.

3. LIMITED WARRANTY — SLEEPING BAGS AND APPAREL
Sleeping bags and apparel carry a 3-year limited warranty against defects such as failed
seams, faulty zippers, and down or insulation migration due to baffle failure. Loft loss
from normal use and compression over time is not considered a defect.

4. LIMITED WARRANTY — FOOTWEAR
Footwear carries a 1-year limited warranty against defects in materials and workmanship,
including sole delamination and seam failure not caused by abnormal wear. Outsole tread
wear and damage from improper fit are not covered.

5. LIMITED WARRANTY — ELECTRONICS AND NAVIGATION
GPS watches and electronic navigation devices carry a 1-year limited warranty against
manufacturing and battery defects. Water damage beyond the device's rated depth, cracked
screens, and battery capacity loss from normal charge cycles are not covered.

6. STOVES AND COOKWARE
Stoves carry a 2-year limited warranty against defects in materials and workmanship.
Clogged jets and corrosion from improper fuel or lack of cleaning are not covered.

7. HOW TO FILE A WARRANTY CLAIM
Submit your order ID, a description of the defect, and clear photos. Our gear team will
evaluate the claim and, if approved, repair the item, replace it, or issue store credit
toward a comparable product. Customers are responsible for shipping the item to us; we
cover return shipping on approved claims.

8. CONTACT
For warranty questions, contact the Trailhead Help Desk at support@trailheadgear.example.

TRAILHEAD GEAR CO. — PRICE MATCH POLICY
Version 1.1 | Effective: 2026-01-01

1. OUR PRICE MATCH PROMISE
If you find a lower advertised price on an identical, in-stock item at an authorized
competitor, we will match it. Price match requests can be made at the time of purchase or
within 14 days after your order is delivered, in which case we refund the difference to
your original payment method.

2. ELIGIBLE COMPETITORS
We match the current advertised price from the following authorized outdoor retailers:
   - REI
   - Backcountry
   - Moosejaw
   - Public Lands
   - The competitor's own website and physical store advertised prices.
The item must be identical in brand, model, size, and color, and must be new, in stock, and
sold and shipped by the competitor directly (not a third-party marketplace seller).

3. EXCLUSIONS
We do not match: prices from third-party marketplace sellers (such as Amazon Marketplace or
eBay), auction sites, membership-only or wholesale club prices, doorbuster or limited-time
flash sales, clearance and closeout prices, open-box or used items, prices that result from
coupon stacking, and pricing errors. Bundle and gift-with-purchase promotions are excluded.

4. SUMMIT CLUB AND PRICE MATCH
Price matches are honored for all customers. Summit Club member rewards points are earned on
the final matched price, not the original price. A price match cannot be combined with a
Summit Club members-only promotional discount on the same item.

5. HOW TO REQUEST A PRICE MATCH
Provide a link or a dated screenshot of the competitor's advertised price showing the item
in stock. Our team verifies the price and availability. Approved at-purchase matches are
applied before checkout; approved post-purchase matches are refunded within 5 to 7 business
days. We reserve the right to verify and to decline requests that do not meet these terms.

6. CONTACT
For price match requests, contact the Trailhead Help Desk at support@trailheadgear.example.

TRAILHEAD GEAR CO. — SUMMIT CLUB MEMBERSHIP PROGRAM
Version 3.0 | Effective: 2026-01-01

1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Summit Club is the Trailhead Gear Co. loyalty program. Members earn points on purchases,
unlock shipping and return perks, and get early access to sales and new gear. There are
three tiers: Base (free), Trail (paid annual), and Summit (paid annual). Points never
expire while your membership is active.

2. BASE TIER (FREE)
Anyone with a Trailhead account is a Base member at no cost. Base members earn 1 point per
$1 spent and receive members-only seasonal promotions. 100 points equals $5 in rewards,
redeemable at checkout. Base members pay standard shipping rates.

3. TRAIL TIER ($39 PER YEAR)
Trail members earn 2 points per $1 spent (an effective 2x rate), receive free standard
shipping on every order with no minimum, and get a 30-day early-access window to seasonal
sales. Trail members also receive one free gear-repair service per year on eligible items.

4. SUMMIT TIER ($99 PER YEAR)
Summit members earn 3 points per $1 spent (an effective 3x rate), receive free expedited
shipping on every order, and get the earliest access to new product drops and sales. Summit
members receive an extended 90-day return window (versus the standard 60 days), a 5% store-
credit bonus on refunds taken as store credit, and two free gear-repair services per year.
Summit members also receive a $20 annual "adventure credit" applied on their membership
anniversary.

5. EARNING AND REDEEMING POINTS
Points are earned on the final purchase price after discounts and price matches, excluding
taxes and shipping. Points post once an order is delivered and the return window-relevant
inspection period begins. Redeem points at checkout in increments of 100 points ($5). Points
are forfeited if a membership lapses for more than 12 months.

6. IS IT WORTH IT
A quick rule of thumb: if you spend more than about $400 per year on gear, Trail tier pays
for itself through the extra points and free shipping alone. If you buy expedited shipping
often or want the extended 90-day return window, Summit tier is the better value. Base tier
is a no-cost way to start earning points immediately.

7. MANAGING YOUR MEMBERSHIP
Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account. Paid tiers renew annually; cancel
before your renewal date to avoid the next charge. Downgrading takes effect at the end of the
current paid term, and you keep your earned points.

8. CONTACT
For membership questions, contact the Trailhead Help Desk at support@trailheadgear.example.
