TRAILHEAD GEAR CO. — SUMMIT CLUB MEMBERSHIP PROGRAM
Version 3.0 | Effective: 2026-01-01

1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Summit Club is the Trailhead Gear Co. loyalty program. Members earn points on purchases,
unlock shipping and return perks, and get early access to sales and new gear. There are
three tiers: Base (free), Trail (paid annual), and Summit (paid annual). Points never
expire while your membership is active.

2. BASE TIER (FREE)
Anyone with a Trailhead account is a Base member at no cost. Base members earn 1 point per
$1 spent and receive members-only seasonal promotions. 100 points equals $5 in rewards,
redeemable at checkout. Base members pay standard shipping rates.

3. TRAIL TIER ($39 PER YEAR)
Trail members earn 2 points per $1 spent (an effective 2x rate), receive free standard
shipping on every order with no minimum, and get a 30-day early-access window to seasonal
sales. Trail members also receive one free gear-repair service per year on eligible items.

4. SUMMIT TIER ($99 PER YEAR)
Summit members earn 3 points per $1 spent (an effective 3x rate), receive free expedited
shipping on every order, and get the earliest access to new product drops and sales. Summit
members receive an extended 90-day return window (versus the standard 60 days), a 5% store-
credit bonus on refunds taken as store credit, and two free gear-repair services per year.
Summit members also receive a $20 annual "adventure credit" applied on their membership
anniversary.

5. EARNING AND REDEEMING POINTS
Points are earned on the final purchase price after discounts and price matches, excluding
taxes and shipping. Points post once an order is delivered and the return window-relevant
inspection period begins. Redeem points at checkout in increments of 100 points ($5). Points
are forfeited if a membership lapses for more than 12 months.

6. IS IT WORTH IT
A quick rule of thumb: if you spend more than about $400 per year on gear, Trail tier pays
for itself through the extra points and free shipping alone. If you buy expedited shipping
often or want the extended 90-day return window, Summit tier is the better value. Base tier
is a no-cost way to start earning points immediately.

7. MANAGING YOUR MEMBERSHIP
Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your account. Paid tiers renew annually; cancel
before your renewal date to avoid the next charge. Downgrading takes effect at the end of the
current paid term, and you keep your earned points.

8. CONTACT
For membership questions, contact the Trailhead Help Desk at support@trailheadgear.example.
